One daily question
for your friend group

FragUns drops a fresh question into your group every day, pings everyone with a push notification, reveals the answers after the deadline — then lets the group banter in the comments.

Open the app View source

Install it: open fraguns.vercel.app in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android) and "Add to Home Screen" — push notifications need the installed app. The interface is in German.

How it works

01

Join a group

Anyone with the invite code joins under a chosen name — no account, no password.

02

A question a day

A daily cron picks one question — free text, a 1–N scale, a poll, or a team split.

03

Answer & get pinged

Web Push notifies the whole group the moment a new question goes live.

04

Reveal & banter

Results unlock after the deadline. Comment, rate the question, react.
